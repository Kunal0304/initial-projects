Indira Vidhya Mandir

Indira Vidhiya Mandir H.H. School(IVMHHS) is a registered society which owns the brand name Indira Vidhiya Mandir(IVM). IVM is the largest chain of schools in India and abroad. IVMS established its first school, in 1941, in the Presidentâ€™s Estate. Post-independence, Naveen Bharat School was set-up and in 1949, was rechristened Indira Vidhiya Mandir, with its present location on Manik bagh Road, indore.

The second IVM was started in Ram Krishna Puram, New Delhi, in 1972. Over the last seven decades, IVM has grown from strength-to-strength, with more than 150 schools, not just in India, but also internationally. IVMS aims to preserve and enhance the highest standards of excellence and prepare generations for the future. The philosophy of IVMS is carefully ingrained in its students, sensitising them to environmental concerns and inculcating in them the feeling of nationalism and communal harmony.
